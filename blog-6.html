INTRODUCTION

In my 3 years as an engineer, the code review process has been my most valuable teacher. Here are five specific comments I've received that fundamentally changed how I think about code.

THE DEEP DIVE

One comment that stuck with me was "This is clever, but I can't explain it to the next person in 10 seconds." It taught me to value clarity over brevity. Another was about error handling—always considering the "unhappy path" first.

If you're the smartest person in the room (or the codebase), you're in the wrong room.

// Instead of clever one-liners:

LESSONS LEARNED

Code review is about the code, not the person. Approaching reviews with humility and a desire to learn is the fastest way to grow.
